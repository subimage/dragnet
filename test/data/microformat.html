Deftones Offering Free Remix Download

Posted on May 26, 2011 by Chris Harris

Deftones

I don’t know how to feel about this.

I actually love the song “You’ve Seen The Butcher,” from the Deftones’ most recent LP, Diamond Eyes. But this…remix? It makes me feel like I should be down in South Beach or something.

Called the “Mustard Pimp remix,” you can hear it here and download it here, too.

This is what you’d hear at a nightclub where The Situation might hold court. This song strips down an amazing rock track, and makes it sounds like Fatboy Slim.

I understand being open-minded and creative and letting other people do their thing, but why the hell would The Deftones let someone remixed this track and turn it into a remixed track that I can picture drunk chicks dancing to in some dimly lit lounge?

Fucking odd. What do you think?
